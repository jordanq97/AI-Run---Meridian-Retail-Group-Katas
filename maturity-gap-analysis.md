## Gap Analysis

### Gap 1

**Dimension:** [AI Capabilities]
**Current level:** [L1]
**Why this gap is most damaging:** [Without effective AI Capabilities the team is not able to maximize efficiency as well as remain competitive in the market as our competitors are adopting AI into their workflows thus setting us at a disadvantage and leading to a potential loss of revenue and new and existing clients.]
**Root cause:** [There is currently no tailored AI learning pathways for each job role within the team.]

---

### Gap 2

**Dimension:** [AI Champions]
**Current level:** [L1]
**Why this gap is most damaging:** [Without an AI champion the team struggles to develop the foundational knowledge of prompting and agent creation. The team is also unmotivated to learn AI as they do not have an AI champion with the knowledge base to support the learning and development of their AI adoption. This will lead to our team being less affective and less appealing to potential clients]
**Root cause:** [There is currently no member of the team with the adequate level of AI adoption required to be labelled an AI champion]
